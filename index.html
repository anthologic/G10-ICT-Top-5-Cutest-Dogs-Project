Top 5 Cutest Dogs

1. Huskies

Huskies have soft fur and wolf-life features that make them adorable.

2. Dalmatian

Dalmatians have black and white spots which create cool patterns.

3. Bulldog

Bulldogs have pinched noses which some people don't like but I think looks adorable.

4. Corgi

Their bodies are like little sausages and their paws are tiny and cute.

5. King Charles

This one is a little odd, but their ears are really floppy and soft and make their eyes look so big!
